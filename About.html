About Our Church

Miracleland Church is a church with a strong community which becomes a second home for its members, a place where friendships flourish and support is abundant. Inspirational leaders guide the congregation with empathy, encouraging members to live out their faith through actions, words, and kindness.

This church is deeply committed to service, reaching out to those in need with a spirit of generosity and compassion that goes beyond its walls. Worship is meaningful and engaging, creating an atmosphere where all can connect deeply with their faith. Teaching is focused, thought-provoking, and rooted in scripture, offering insights that members can apply to their daily lives. The church nurtures personal growth, encouraging each individual to explore and deepen their spirituality, strengthen their character, and live with purpose. United by a shared mission and clear goals, this church stands as a beacon of hope, faith, and purpose in the community.

Our Offficials

BISHOP

Bishop Thomas Madara is a senior member of the Christian clergy who is responsible for overseeing Miracleland ministries within Mombasa. He holds the authority to ordain priests, administer sacraments, and provide spiritual leadership and guidance to their congregations.

OVERSEAR

Mrs Beatrice Olando supervises or manages others, often in a work or project setting of the church. Her role is to ensure that tasks are completed correctly and efficiently.

PASTOR

Pst. Beatrice Olando is a religious leader, responsible for providing spiritual guidance, teaching, and support to a our church. The role includes preaching sermons, leading worship services, offering pastoral care (such as counseling and support), and guiding the spiritual growth of the community.
